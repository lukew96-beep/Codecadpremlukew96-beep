There was a time when the land filled with rollercoasters and screams was a wild safari adventure park and they took extreme measures to insure interesting species. They were willing to go as far as it took.

You CREATED WHAT?!

The doctors in the biolab were there three hours earlier on January 15, 1975. While the rest of the safari workers were at home these eager scientist were playing god in a way that would have terrible consequences. A wolf sat on the operation table completely unaware of what was about to be done to it. Three other wolves laid on obersevation tables to each side. Beside the observation tables was the instructions for a wolf, dog hibrid. A cross between wolves and routerilers.

  1. Extract blood from canine specimine
  2. Spin blood in centrifuge for aproximately thirty seconds.
  3. Inject blood into wolf species of exact blood type after adding three CCs of Adrenaline

One of the scientists injected the first specimine and it imediately began to convulse uncontrollably. "We need 30 CCs of valium stat!", shouted the frantic vet. In the rocking the button control for the other spicimins got hit as well. "Great, hell! WHAT have we done?!"
